The Quarter Lotus

Here you can sit on your meditation seat with your legs loosely crossed and both feet resting below the opposite thigh or knee.
From the top of your head to your toes.

The Method:

  • Mind to sky.
  • Neck relaxed
  • Spine straight with tail bone tucked in, hold but relax the stomach.
  • Legs sinking down forming the root of your posture.
  • Feet soft, and relax the ankle.
  • Now relax the hole body again.
  • Breath. Inhale..... Exhale
